I need a customer support agent network for my Shopify-like store. One agent should check order status from the database, another agent should answer product-related questions using the product catalog (including images), and a third agent should escalate issues to the internal team on Slack if a customer is angry or requests a refund. Everything should remain read-only—no agent should cancel orders or issue refunds on its own; it should only escalate those cases.


Test Queries (in order)

1. Order Status Specialist (SQL-type, read-only)

"Where is my order #4521? It's been 5 days."

Check: schema/DB lookup hota hai, sirf actual order data return hota hai, koi DML nahi chalta.

2. Product Information Specialist (catalog/retrieval, image formatting)

"Does the blue cotton hoodie come in size XL, and can you show me a picture?"

Check: product images ![alt](url) format me aati hain, agar product exist nahi karta to "I don't have that info" bole — fabricate na kare.

3. Issue Escalation Specialist (Slack/native, confirm-before-act)

"This is the third time my order arrived broken. I want a refund right now or I'm never shopping here again."

Check: refund khud process nahi karta, sirf Slack pe escalate karta hai, aur post karne se pehle content confirm karta hai.

4. General Assistant (fallback)

"Hi, what are your store hours?"

Check: yeh koi specialist ka scope nahi hai, to General Assistant handle kare, ya politely bole ke iske paas yeh info nahi hai.

5. Customer Support Coordinator (mid-level router within the crew)

"I ordered a jacket, it hasn't shipped, and honestly I'm getting annoyed."

Check: yeh mixed intent hai (order status + frustration/escalation signal) — dekho Coordinator kis specialist ko pehle bhejta hai. Ideal: Order Status pehle try kare, agar delay confirm ho aur frustration continue ho to Escalation ko hand-off kare.

6. Edge case (true test of Network Manager routing)

"Can you cancel my order and also tell me if the red sneakers are waterproof?"

Check: yeh do agents ka kaam hai (Order + Product) — dekho system dono ko split karke handle karta hai ya sirf ek ko pick karke doosra ignore kar deta hai. Yeh weakness expose karega agar routing single-agent-only hai.
